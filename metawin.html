Biography

Born in Vancouver, Canada, Win Metawin displayed an early passion for the environment and wildlife. Inspired by his biologist parents, he spent much of his childhood exploring the natural beauty of British Columbia. He pursued his passion further by obtaining a B.Sc. in Environmental Science from the Harvard University, followed by a M.Sc in Sustainable Development from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). He is now pursuing his Ph.D. in De La Salle University.
