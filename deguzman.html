Biography

Joaquin de Guzman grew up in the historic city of Vigan, Ilocos Sur, where he was surrounded by a rich tapestry of Philippine culture and heritage. From a young age, Joaquin was fascinated by the intricate Spanish colonial architecture of his hometown, which sparked his interest in engineering and design. Encouraged by his family, who recognized his potential early on, Joaquin excelled in mathematics and physics throughout his school years, leading him to choose civil engineering as his career path.
